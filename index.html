💡 Quick Testing Walkthrough

  1. Click Open Portal to load the React landing page, then click Get Started.
  2. Sign Up as a Recruiter (Company) ➔ Navigate to Post a Vacancy ➔ Publish a job post (e.g. Software Engineer Intern at Google).
  3. Log out and click Sign Up to create a Student Candidate account.
  4. Fill in the Degree & Qualifications form. This verifies your credentials.
  5. In Explore Vacancies, select the job you posted, fill in the Cover Note, and click Submit Application.
  6. Log back into your Recruiter account ➔ Go to Applicants List ➔ Click Offer Call Letter ➔ Input time coordinates. This sends the formal call letter to the candidate's dashboard inbox and logs the email transmission in the backend terminal console.
